Kali Linux & Network Security

Project research on Kali Linux tool BetterCAP, part of my Diploma in Cybersecurity Fundamentals.

Read more

I invite you to explore my project research by clicking here.
Welcome to my project! I want to share with you my exploration of Kali Linux, an incredible open-source operating system that has revolutionized the world of cybersecurity. With over 350 penetration testing tools specifically designed for computer forensics and ethical hacking, Kali Linux is a game-changer in this field.
In this research, I dive into the intriguing world of BetterCAP, a powerful program within Kali Linux. BetterCAP empowers ethical hackers and security professionals to perform sophisticated man-in-the-middle attacks, manipulate network traffic, and even extract passwords. It's a remarkable tool that showcases the potential and complexity of cybersecurity.
Throughout this project, I explore the concept of man-in-the-middle attacks and shed light on their primary objective: the theft of sensitive information. I discuss the various network attacks that can be executed using BetterCAP, including ARP spoofing, domain request redirection, HTTPS bypassing, and code injection. It's truly fascinating to uncover the intricacies of these techniques.
Finally, I delve into the broader implications of using penetration testing tools like BetterCAP. Beyond the technical aspects, I examine the legal, ethical, economic, and social consequences that arise from the responsible use of such powerful tools.
Thank you for joining me on this captivating journey into the world of cybersecurity!
